About Sam

Sam is an intuitive psychic and gifted tarot reader who blends mysticism with music and magic. With years of experience reading the cards and connecting to energies beyond the veil, Sam provides empowering, heartfelt insight to those seeking guidance. She has a deep understanding of astrology and uses her knowledge and intution to guide you through your toughest questions and deepest paths of your life.

Beyond the deck, Sam is also a member doing vocals and instrumentation of the band Precognition—a project where haunting lyrics and ethereal melodies intertwine to tell stories of the unseen.

Whether through voice or vision, Sam’s art invites you to explore the mysteries of your soul.
